About Project

Welcome to the cocktail culture website.

Here we will show you the world's most popular cocktails that have stood the test of time. We will also tell you their recipes and cooking methods.

This site was created as an HTML / CSS project to demonstrate cocktail skills and basic web development skills. Immerse yourself in our classic cocktail collection.

Reveal the secrets of each drink with detailed recipes, lists of ingredients and instructions. From a refreshing mojito to an exquisite martini, we have cocktails for every taste.

Join our cocktail community, share your creations in the site section "Contact" and take your mixing skills to the next level.

We wish you a pleasant time and a pleasant drink with us!
